ELEVATE YOUR HEART RATE

Our state-of-the-art cardio floor is designed to burn calories, boost endurance, and push your limits. Discover our premium machines below.

CARDIO MACHINES

World-class equipment for maximum calorie burn and cardiovascular health.

Treadmill

The ultimate classic for running and walking. Our high-tech treadmills offer built-in screens, heart rate tracking, and dynamic incline settings to simulate real-world terrain.

How to use:

Start with a brisk 5-minute walk to warm up. Gradually increase the speed or incline for high-intensity intervals (HIIT), or maintain a steady pace for endurance.

Elliptical Cross-Trainer

A low-impact alternative to running that targets both upper and lower body. Perfect for protecting your joints while still getting an intense sweat session.

How to use:

Keep your posture upright and engage your core. Push and pull the handles simultaneously with your leg strides for a full-body cardiovascular workout.

Stair Climber

An intense lower-body burner. The infinite staircase forces you to work against gravity, building massive strength in your glutes, quads, and calves.

How to use:

Avoid leaning heavily on the handrails. Stand tall, step evenly, and drive through your heels to maximize glute activation. Start slow to build stamina.

Rowing Machine

A powerhouse machine that works 86% of your body's muscles in a single stroke. It combines strength and cardio for the ultimate calorie-torching workout.

How to use:

Focus on the sequence: Push with your legs, lean back slightly, then pull the handle to your chest. Reverse the motion to return. Power comes from the legs!

Stationary Bike

Whether you prefer upright or recumbent styles, our bikes offer a fantastic cardiovascular challenge with adjustable resistance for hill climbs and sprints.

How to use:

Adjust the seat so your knee has a slight bend at the bottom of the pedal stroke. Use heavy resistance for strength building or light resistance for speed work.

Assault Air Bike

The harder you push, the more resistance it provides. The air bike is notorious for its brutal efficiency in HIIT workouts and metabolic conditioning.

How to use:

Go all out for short bursts (e.g., 10-20 seconds), using both arms and legs aggressively, followed by a brief rest period. It's meant for maximum effort.

STRENGTH EQUIPMENT

Build raw power and sculpt your physique with our premium free weights and machines.

Dumbbell Rack

Our massive free weight area features complete dumbbell sets ranging from 5 lbs to 150 lbs. Perfect for isolating muscles and correcting imbalances.

How to use:

Select an appropriate weight. Ensure a firm grip and controlled movement during exercises like chest presses, bicep curls, or shoulder raises. Re-rack when finished!

Power / Squat Rack

The centerpiece of any strength routine. Features adjustable safety pins and J-hooks for heavy compound movements like squats and overhead presses.

How to use:

Set the J-hooks to shoulder height. Unrack the barbell, step back, and perform your squat with a straight back and engaged core. Always use safety pins!

Bench Press Station

Dedicated stations for flat, incline, and decline barbell bench presses to target all areas of the pectorals, triceps, and anterior deltoids.

How to use:

Lie flat with eyes under the bar. Grip slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower the bar to your chest with control, then press up powerfully. Use a spotter for heavy sets.

Cable Crossover

Highly versatile multi-station machine providing constant tension. Great for chest flyes, tricep pushdowns, face pulls, and endless accessory work.

How to use:

Adjust the pulley height to match your exercise. Select a manageable weight on the stack. Maintain a slight bend in your joints and focus on the muscle squeeze.

YOGA & WELLNESS

Find your balance, improve flexibility, and connect your mind and body.

Premium Mats

Extra-thick, non-slip yoga mats providing the perfect foundation for all poses, from resting Savasana to challenging inversions.

How to use:

Unroll in a clear space. Use for core work, stretching, and guided flows. Wipe down with our provided sanitizing stations after your session.

Support Blocks

High-density foam blocks designed to safely assist with alignment, deepen stretches, and provide extra height when needed.

How to use:

Place under your hands, feet, or seat. Great for modifying difficult poses (like Half Moon or Triangle) so you maintain perfect form without straining.

Resistance Bands

A variety of tension bands excellent for physical therapy, joint mobility, and adding light resistance to pilates or yoga routines.

How to use:

Wrap around your feet or hold in your hands to gently pull yourself deeper into stretches, or use them to activate your glutes and shoulders.

Recovery Rollers

Essential for post-workout recovery. High-density and textured foam rollers help release muscle knots and improve blood circulation.

How to use:

Place under tight muscles (like calves, quads, or back) and slowly roll back and forth. Pause on tender spots for 20-30 seconds to release tension.
